Top 3 Questions

1. How do I adopt a pet?

Thinking of adopting? That is fantastic! We find homes for over 25,000 pets each year. Learn how to adopt a pet and give a pet a forever home.

2. Where can I get help re-homing a pet or putting a pet up for adoption?

First, check with family members or friends. While PAC Charities™ of USA doesn’t take in pets directly, we partner with over 250 groups that do. Call your local animal welfare organization or shelter.

3. How do I make a donation and where does it go?

We are so glad you asked! Help us adopt out even more pets and provide critical funding to animal welfare groups. Donate now or connect with us for more information.

All donations to PAC Charities of USA stay in USA. Your contributions are combined with those of others across the country to save the lives of homeless pets. Learn more about where your donations go.
