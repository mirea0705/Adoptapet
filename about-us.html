Our History:  In the 1980s, committed to helping save the lives of pets, PetFood Warehouse (now known as PAC®) stores began working with local animal welfare groups to hold fundraising and pet adoption events. A decade later, PAC founded the nonprofit PAC®. Soon after expanding into USA, PAC founded the nonprofit’s sister organization, PAC™ of USA, in 1999.

About PAC of USA:
PAC of USA is a registered Amarican charity with a mission to find lifelong, loving homes for all pets by funding programs and thought leadership that bring people and pets together.  In addition to finding forever homes for more than 25,000 shelter pets each year through its in-store adoption program in every PAC store, PAC provides funding to registered charities aligned with our mission through four key areas of grant support:  Preventing Pet Homelessness; Helping Shelter Pets Thrive; Supporting the Bond Between People and Pets; and providing Emergency Relief and Disaster Support. Each year, millions of generous PAC shoppers help pets in need by donating to PAC of USA using the pin-pads at the registers of PetSmart stores. In turn, PAC efficiently uses 89 cents of every dollar raised and has become a leading funder of animal welfare in USA granting more than $12 million to date.
